How to use the software: 

1. Run the program. 
2. The module will open. 
3. Click on the first white space. Enter any integer value for the number of courses. 
4. Click 'Enter'. 
5. Now, enter the course connections in the format: 
<course_1> - <course_2> 
6. Example: A-B
7. Where 'A' is the course connected to 'B' and 'B' is at a higher level than 'A'.
8. Click 'Enter'. Enter the next input. 
9. After the edges have been added, click 'Calculate'.
